About the Artist

Nice to meet you I’m Karina Carrero. I’m currently a student at Queens College working towards an B.F.A in Design. My passion is Illustrating and designing. Some of my other hobbies are sewing and cooking.

Just a little more about me I was born and rasied in the Bronx. I have been drawing since I was very young but it was till I went to college where I took my first printmaking class when I decied I was going to pursure art as my career path.
