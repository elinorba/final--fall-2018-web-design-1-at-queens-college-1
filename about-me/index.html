Who Am I?

My name is Julia Martinez. I'm currently attending Queens College and majoring in BFA Design. I've completed a few course in Animation and Fine Arts at a different school I transferred from; I was double majoring in both programs for two years until I moved.

Taking course through each programs lead me to experience different medias and create something different through it. I have learned many softwares and different medias in art, such as printing, photography, painting (oil, acrylic, and water colors) and sculpting (ceramic and clay). I enjoy creating new pieces and having different options in techniques and media, however I was more drawn to animation and story.
