Elinor Aharon is a young and motivated social media coordinator in NYC with experience in digital brand marketing, development, content strategy and communications.

Raised in Queens, New York, she has developed a strong understating for culture, entertainment, and technology that has helped build her passion for growing and defining brands in the digital realm.

As an intern at various companies, she assisted in building digital communities for various brands that allowed her to learn how to succeed in brand outreach and development, while also curating content that caters to large, yet specific audiences.

Currently, she works as a Social Media Cordinator at Talent Resources, managing 4 social accounts, and serves as the Head of Digital Strategy for HGR Group.

She is also completing her final year at Queens College with a major in Media Studies and Sociology and a minor in Graphic Design.
